What is NOIR?

NOIR is built around a simple belief. We sell fragrances — some are original creations, some are inspirations, and some are quality interpretations of beloved classics. We are not here to tell you what you should wear. If you want the original fragrance, we encourage it. We simply ask a different question: beyond the label, what does the scent do? NOIR exists to help people discover fragrances more easily, save time, explore confidently, and find scents that genuinely fit their personality and lifestyle. Welcome to NOIR.

Who is NOIR for?

NOIR is for the person becoming someone. The one who understands that the scent you choose speaks before you do — in every room, every matatu, every first impression. A fragrance is more than its label; strip the brand away and what remains is the trail you leave behind. That trail is what we follow. Original or quality interpretation, you're here for the same thing — to be remembered. Brand or no brand. We respect your choice, and we make sure you leave smelling like the person you're working to become.
